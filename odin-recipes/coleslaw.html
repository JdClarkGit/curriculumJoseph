Description

This is a coleslaw recipe that utilizes the sweetness of sugar as well as the twang of lemon juice and vinegar all to create a coherent, delicious piece of coleslaw

Coleslaw Dressing
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  • Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  • Step 2: Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  • Step 3: Store in airlight container
